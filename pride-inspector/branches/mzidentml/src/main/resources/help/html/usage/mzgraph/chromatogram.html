Chromatogram Browser

Previous | Next | See Also

Chromatogram Browser allows you gain an insight on the chromatogram you interested, the main display area shows the chromatogram which you can interact with. On right hand side of the browser, there is also a toolbar which offers a number of convenient commands.

Chromatogram Browser offers a list of features, you can Save, Print, Export, Zoom, Show Grid.

Save: On the tool bar, click on , you can save the chromatogram to your disk as a PNG image.

Print: On the tool bar, click on , you can print the chromatogram as a PNG image.

Export: On the tool bar, click on , you can export the time and intensity values into a tab delimited file.

Zoom: To zoom in, you can left click and hold your mouse key, drag to highlight the area you want to zoom into. To zoom out, you can either right click your mouse in the main display area or on the tool bar, click on .

Show Grid: On the tool bar, click on , this will show/hide the grid in the main display area.
