How does PRIDE Inspector calculate delta m/z?

Previous | Next | See Also

Four types of information have been taken into account to calculate delta m/z in PRIDE Inspector:

  • Peptide Sequence - Required
  • Post translational modifications - Optional
  • Precursor m/z - Required
  • Precursor charge - Required

The calculation can be broken down into the following steps:

1. Calculate peptide mass: simply sum the masses of all the amino acids in the peptide.

2. Calculate PTM mass: simply sum the masses of all the post translational modifications in the peptide.

3. Calculate theoretical mass: [Peptide mass - PTM mass - 18 dalton].

4. Calculate precursor mass: [(Precursor m/z * Precursor charge) - (Precursor charge * 1 dalton)].

5. Calculate detla m/z: [(Precursor mass - Theoretical mass)/Precursor charge].
