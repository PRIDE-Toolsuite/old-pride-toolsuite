Protein Sequence Viewer

Previous | Next | See Also

Protein Sequence Viewer is used for visualizing protein sequence, and highlight peptide sequences and PTMs.

The top section of Protein Sequence Viewer shows some details about the protein, such as: protein name, protein sequence coverage.

As can be seen from the screenshot below, both peptides and PTMs are highlighted in the protein sequences using different colour. Strict-fit peptides are in light green, fuzzy-fit peptides are in brown colour, and PTMs are in red, peptides which are overlapping are highlighted using dark green.
